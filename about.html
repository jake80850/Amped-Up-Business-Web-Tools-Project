About Us

Our Story

Amped Up is a vibrant music festival that blends the soulful rhythms of R&B with the fresh sounds of indie music, creating a dynamic experience for fans of both genres. The festival brings together smooth vocals, heartfelt lyrics, and groovy beats from rising R&B artists, while also showcasing the creativity and raw energy of indie bands.

With a mix of intimate performances, crowd-swaying anthems, and a welcoming community vibe, Amped Up offers something for everyone—whether you’re there to chill and vibe out or to discover your next favorite artist. It’s more than just a concert; it’s a celebration of sound, connection, and artistry.

Organizers

Goldenvoice is the visionary organizer behind our festival, renowned for producing some of the most iconic live music experiences in the world. As a leading concert and festival promoter, Goldenvoice brings decades of expertise in curating unforgettable events that celebrate music, art, and community.
