Q&A

Quick answers to the most common AMPED UP 26 questions.

Is re-entry allowed if I leave the festival grounds?
For safety reasons, re-entry is not permitted once you leave the festival. Please plan ahead and bring everything you need before entering.
What items am I allowed to bring into the festival?
You may bring essentials such as empty reusable water bottles, small bags (under 12”×12”), hats, sunscreen, and personal medications (with prescription labels). A full list of permitted and prohibited items can be found on our Rules & Safety page.
Will food and drinks be available for purchase inside the festival?
Yes! A variety of food vendors, including vegetarian and vegan options, will be available. Alcoholic beverages will be served to guests 21+ with valid government-issued ID. Free water refill stations are also located throughout the grounds.
Are there medical services on-site?
Yes, trained medical staff and first aid stations will be available throughout the festival grounds for any emergencies or health concerns.
How do I contact Lost & Found?
Lost & Found will be located near the main entrance. After the festival, unclaimed items can be reported or retrieved through our online Lost & Found form.
Is parking available at the festival?
Yes, parking lots will be available near the venue. ADA parking is also provided with valid placards. We encourage carpooling or using public transportation whenever possible.
