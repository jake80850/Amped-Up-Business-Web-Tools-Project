Lineup

Friday – R&B & Night Vibes
Kehlani (hometown headliner) tops a full R&B night with H.E.R., Goapele, Marc E. Bassy, Destin Conrad, Thuy, Soul Train DJs, Kefifo & Friends, and a Local Rotation Bay R&B showcase.

Saturday – EDM & Night Run
ILLENIUM B2B DABIN close the festival with a special set, joined by San Holo, Gryffin, ARMNHMR, Jai Wolf, Porter Robinson, Slander, TIESTO, Seven Lions, and more guests.

Tickets

AMPED UP 26 is limited-capacity for a true festival feel. Choose from GA, GA + Parking, VIP, and single-day options, with weekend passes for the full two-day experience.

Once a tier sells out, it’s gone—secure your pass early and build your weekend around your must-see sets.

The Experience

From hydration hubs and Kandi crafting tables to bass-pumping DJ sets, laser-lit chill zones, street food pop-ups, and more!, the entire space is built like a mini-festival playground.

General Information

Venue address, gate times, parking details, and full festival policies will be announced with the complete lineup drop. AMPED UP 26 is a two-day outdoor festival in the Bay Area with re-entry rules, security screening, and on-site medical staff.
